How It All Began...

While I was still fresh into my final year of graduate school I came across a VICE news article on the Femthiests. After doing further research I thought to my self, "why in the hell has no one tried to make a book or movie or tv show out of this extremist group... I decided that I would go ahead and assume that task by breaking it into a trilogy. By mixing it with many of my own life experiences I feel I have been able to create an epic start to a series that is going to just get better with the sequel- The Last Man: Rise of the Pink Army and fantastic with the finale to the trilogy- The Last Man: Final Bout. I intended this book for 16 to 50, basically anyone who likes controversy, loves satire, and is sick of the bs PC culture western civilization has dissolved into.
